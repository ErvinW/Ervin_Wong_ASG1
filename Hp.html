Companion app: No
Active Noise cancelling(ANC): No
Battery life: NA
IPX Rating: NA

The Xbox Series X/S headset's thick padding on the headband and earcups provide maximum comfort for long gaming sessions, while its mic is able to capture users' voices clearly over calls and voice chat. The built-in mic cannot be retracted, but can be folded around the earcup and can be turned on and off via a swtich on the side of the headset.
